# 10 Risk Factors — Scoring Criteria

## Factor 1: Dx-Proc Mismatch (0-10)
- 0: Perfect match
- 5: Partial match (related but not exact)
- 10: Complete mismatch (procedure not justified by diagnosis)

## Factor 2: CC/MCC Completeness (0-10)
- 0: All documented conditions coded
- 5: Some conditions not coded
- 10: Major conditions missing (e.g., sepsis documented but not coded)

## Factor 3: LOS Outlier (0-10)
- 0: LOS within expected range for DRG
- 5: LOS 1-2 days beyond trim point
- 10: LOS >3 days beyond trim point without justification

## Factor 4: Documentation Gaps (0-10)
- 0: All required documents present
- 5: Minor documents missing
- 10: Critical documents missing (operative note, pathology)

## Factor 5: Timing Violations (0-10)
- 0: Submitted on time, authen code valid
- 5: Close to deadline
- 10: Late submission or no authen code

## Factor 6: Drug Catalog Compliance (0-10)
- 0: All drugs match FDH catalog
- 5: Minor mismatches
- 10: Multiple drugs not in catalog

## Factor 7: Device/Implant Documentation (0-10)
- 0: Complete lot/serial/GPO match
- 5: Partial documentation
- 10: No implant documentation

## Factor 8: DRG Weight Anomaly (0-10)
- 0: RW within expected range
- 5: RW slightly high for diagnosis
- 10: RW significantly above expected (audit trigger)

## Factor 9: Historical Pattern (0-10)
- 0: This type rarely denied (<5%)
- 5: Moderate deny rate (5-15%)
- 10: High deny rate historically (>15%)

## Factor 10: Payer-Specific Rules (0-10)
- 0: Fully compliant with payer rules
- 5: Minor compliance gaps
- 10: Major payer-specific rule violation
