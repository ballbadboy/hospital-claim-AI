# Financial Simulation Models

## Model 1: Add Beds
- Additional Revenue = New Beds × Occupancy Rate × Avg Revenue per Bed Day × 365
- Additional Staff = New Beds × 0.5 FTE nurses + 0.1 FTE doctors + 0.3 FTE support
- Capex = New Beds × ฿500K-1M per bed (construction + equipment)
- Breakeven = Capex / (Additional Revenue - Additional Operating Cost) × 12 months

## Model 2: Salary Adjustment
- Cost Increase = Total Nurse Salary × % Increase × 12
- Turnover Reduction = ~5-8% turnover decrease per 10% salary increase (research-based)
- Savings from Reduced Turnover = Avoided Turnover × ฿200K per nurse (recruitment + training)
- Net Impact = Savings - Cost Increase

## Model 3: Payer Mix Shift
- Revenue Delta = Volume × (New Avg Revenue per Case - Old Avg Revenue per Case)
- Avg Revenue: Private ฿25K/case, SSO ฿8K/case, UC ฿7K/case
- Marketing Cost for Private = ฿500K-2M/year

## Model 4: New Service Line
- Revenue = Cases/month × Avg Revenue/case × Utilization Rate
- Scenarios: 50% / 75% / 100% utilization
- Capex: equipment + renovation + recruitment
- Breakeven: typically 18-36 months

## Model 5: Reduce ALOS
- Beds Freed = Current Admissions × Days Reduced / 365
- Additional Capacity = Freed Beds × Occupancy Rate × Revenue per Day
- Implementation Cost: discharge planning staff, process improvement

## Model 6: Drug Cost Optimization
- Savings = Drug Spend × % Switchable × Avg Price Difference
- Typical: 20-30% of drugs switchable, 40-60% price difference
- Annual Savings = ฿2-10M for 100-bed hospital
