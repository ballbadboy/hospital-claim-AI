# Triage Criteria — เกณฑ์คัดแยกผู้ป่วยฉุกเฉิน

## 🔴 Red — Resuscitation (ฉุกเฉินวิกฤต)
- Cardiac arrest / Respiratory arrest
- Severe shock (SBP < 90, altered consciousness)
- Airway obstruction
- Massive hemorrhage
- Status epilepticus
- Severe anaphylaxis
→ **UCEP eligible** — เข้า รพ.ไหนก็ได้ 72 ชม.

## 🟠 Orange — Emergency (ฉุกเฉินเร่งด่วน)
- Chest pain with cardiac features
- Stroke symptoms (onset < 4.5 hrs)
- Major trauma (ISS ≥ 16)
- Severe bleeding (hemodynamically unstable)
- Acute abdomen with peritonitis
- Severe burns > 20% TBSA
→ **UCEP eligible**

## 🟡 Yellow — Urgent (เร่งด่วน)
- Moderate illness needing prompt care
- Simple fracture
- Acute abdomen (stable)
- High fever with risk factors
- Moderate asthma exacerbation
→ **Not UCEP** — ใช้สิทธิปกติ

## 🟢 Green — Non-urgent
- Minor illness/injury
- Chronic condition follow-up
- Prescription refill
→ **Not UCEP, Not ER claim**
