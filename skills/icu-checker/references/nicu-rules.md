# NICU-Specific Rules

## Birthweight DRG Categories (Pre-MDC)
| Weight | DRG Group | Typical RW |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| <750g | Extreme Low Birth Weight | Very High |
| 750-999g | Very Low Birth Weight | High |
| 1000-1499g | Low Birth Weight | Moderate-High |
| 1500-1999g | Moderate Low Birth Weight | Moderate |
| 2000-2499g | Near Term | Low-Moderate |
| ≥2500g | Normal Weight (if problems) | Standard |

## Required Documentation
- Birthweight (grams) — determines DRG
- Gestational age (weeks)
- APGAR score at 1 min and 5 min
- Mode of delivery

## Major Neonatal Problems (affect DRG)
- RDS (Respiratory Distress Syndrome) — P22.0
- NEC (Necrotizing Enterocolitis) — P77
- IVH (Intraventricular Hemorrhage) — P52.x
- PDA (Patent Ductus Arteriosus) — Q25.0
- BPD (Bronchopulmonary Dysplasia) — P27.1
