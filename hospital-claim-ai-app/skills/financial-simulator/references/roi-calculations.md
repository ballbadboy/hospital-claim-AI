# ROI Calculation Guide

## Simple ROI
ROI = (Net Benefit / Investment Cost) × 100%

## Payback Period
Payback = Investment / Annual Net Benefit (months)

## NPV (simplified)
NPV = Σ (Annual Benefit / (1 + discount_rate)^year) - Investment
Use discount rate 8-10% for Thai hospitals

## Risk Levels
- Low: ROI > 30%, Payback < 12 months
- Medium: ROI 15-30%, Payback 12-24 months
- High: ROI < 15%, Payback > 24 months
