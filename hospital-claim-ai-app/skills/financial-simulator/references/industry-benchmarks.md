# Thai Private Hospital Benchmarks

| Metric | Small (<100 beds) | Medium (100-300) | Large (>300) |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|--------------|
| Revenue/Bed/Year | ฿2-4M | ฿3-5M | ฿4-8M |
| Operating Margin | 5-10% | 8-15% | 10-20% |
| Nurse:Bed Ratio | 1:2-3 | 1:2-3 | 1:1.5-2 |
| Drug Cost % | 25-35% | 20-30% | 18-25% |
| AR Days | 50-70 | 40-60 | 35-50 |
| Bed Occupancy | 50-70% | 60-80% | 70-85% |
| ALOS | 3-5 days | 3-4 days | 3-5 days |
| Staff/Bed | 3-4 | 4-5 | 5-7 |
