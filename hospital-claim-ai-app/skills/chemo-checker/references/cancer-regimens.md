# Common Cancer Regimens by Type

## Breast Cancer (C50.x)
| Regimen | Drugs | Indication |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| AC-T | Doxorubicin + Cyclophosphamide → Paclitaxel | Standard adjuvant |
| CMF | Cyclophosphamide + Methotrexate + 5-FU | Alternative adjuvant |
| Herceptin | Trastuzumab | HER2+ only (need HER2 test) |

## Colorectal (C18-C20)
| Regimen | Drugs | Indication |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| FOLFOX | 5-FU + Leucovorin + Oxaliplatin | Stage III adjuvant |
| FOLFIRI | 5-FU + Leucovorin + Irinotecan | Metastatic |
| XELOX | Capecitabine + Oxaliplatin | Alternative to FOLFOX |

## Lung (C34.x)
| Regimen | Drugs | Indication |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| Cisplatin-Gemcitabine | Standard NSCLC | No driver mutation |
| Gefitinib/Erlotinib | EGFR TKI | EGFR mutation+ |
| Crizotinib | ALK inhibitor | ALK rearrangement+ |

## Lymphoma (C81-C85)
| Regimen | Drugs | Indication |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| CHOP | Cyclophosphamide + Doxorubicin + Vincristine + Prednisolone | NHL |
| R-CHOP | Rituximab + CHOP | CD20+ B-cell NHL |
| ABVD | Doxorubicin + Bleomycin + Vinblastine + Dacarbazine | Hodgkin |
