# Board Report Writing Guidelines

## What Board/CEO Cares About
1. **Money** — Revenue, margin, cash position
2. **Risk** — Quality issues, legal exposure, staffing crisis
3. **Growth** — New services, volume trends, market position
4. **Action** — What needs to be decided THIS meeting

## Presentation Tips
- Lead with exceptions (what changed, what's concerning)
- Use RAG (Red/Amber/Green) for every KPI
- Max 3 action items per meeting
- Include MoM and YoY trends (not just absolute numbers)
- One page executive summary — details in appendix

## Common Mistakes
- Too much data, not enough insight
- No comparison (benchmark, last month, last year)
- Action items without owners/deadlines
- Burying bad news in middle of report
